What is Earth Day?

Earth day is a once a year event created to help spread awareness on all the negative things we as humans do to the Earth. These include burning fossil fuels, littering, wasting, and many others. It is also a day to encourage civic action and political action.

Here is a list of some things you can do during Earth Day:

  • Take your pet for a walk
  • Enjoy Nature
  • Pick up some garbage on a beach
  • Join a Parade
  • Help plant a tree
