What are People Doing on Earth Day?

During Earth Day people parade around to spread awareness about the Earth with the hope to be able to change something about the Earth for the better.
Earth day doesn't necessarily last only 24 hours. Some people take the time to make a bigger change, something along the lines of planting trees or other various plants over a week or maybe even a month.
There are many ways you can also participate in Earth day, whether it be donating to one of many non-profit organizations or picking up some plastic waste. If you want you can even create a garden for yourself or go for a walk in a forest to enjoy Earth.
